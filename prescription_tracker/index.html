Who Will Benefit?

This site is for people who need help keeping track of multiple prescriptions. They will be able to add the prescription, who it is for, what pharmacy fills it, how many refills are left, and when the next refill is due.

Who Benefits Most?

This website will be especially helpful for seniors, and families with multiple people taking medications. It can be easy to lose track of your prescriptions, but imagine being able to pull up a website, see all of your prescriptions on one page, and know exactly which ones need refills, and which pharmacy fills it! You can add an unlimited number of medications with an unlimited number of people who take them.

No More Worries!

Of all the things we have to worry about every day, our medications should not be on that list. Giving up the mental load that medications cause allows for more stress-free interactions with your doctor and your pharmacist.
