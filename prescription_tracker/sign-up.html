User Information

User

The user will need to create an account, which will allow their information to be safely stored away from prying eyes. The prescription information is stored on our secure servers, and we never sell any of your information.

Stored Info

This app will need to store names, medications, pharmacies, and other pertinent information. You may request that it be deleted at any time. If you cancel your account, your information will be permanently deleted from our servers. CAUTION: If you delete your account, you will need to enter all information again. If you wish to delete your account, or have any questions or concerns, please contact the developer at cha22016@byui.edu.
